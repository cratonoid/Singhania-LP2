🎓 Scholarships & Financial Aid

Singhania University offers a robust financial aid system to ensure education remains accessible and inclusive.

Merit-Based

Up to 75% concession based on 10+2 or entrance scores (JEE/CAT/MAT).

Sports Achievers

100% tuition waiver for national/state/district-level sports achievers.

President’s Scholarship

35% fee concession for female students and those without a father.

Government Aid

Available through central/state schemes like SJED and MOMA.

Note: Merit scholarships apply for the first year only; continuation depends on academic performance.
